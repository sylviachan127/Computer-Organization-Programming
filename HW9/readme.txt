You can shoot up to 10 bullets at a time, and no more bullets can be shoot util 10 bullets inside the frame has been clean.  Your goal is to clear out all the blue blocks while avoid the rainbow bullets that are shoots out by Evil White while time remains.  When all 6 of your lives are lose or if time runs out, the game ends.  

Controls:

left and right: control user's movement
Up: Shoot bullets
