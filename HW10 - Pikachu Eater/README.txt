In this game, you have the chance to become a pikachu.  Your goal is to eat as much candies as you can.  You will level up every time your score reaches a score up a mulitple of 15.  Use left, right, up, and down to control your pikachu movement.  Every time a level start, you get a grace time period for you to adjust where all the candy eaters are located at.  When your pikachu collides with a candy eater, you lose a life.   When all your lifes are lose, your game is over.  In addition, when you level up to a multiplue of 3, you gain a life.  For every level going up, one more candy eater appears on screen.  The max amount of candy eaters appear on screen is set to 25.  When this max number is reach, additional life will not be gain.
